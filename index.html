MY NAME IS RIDWAN AND THIS IS ME.

My name is Ridwan Alawode. I am a Nigerian Yoruba Muslim and I live in the city of Offa, Kwara State. A chronic introvert with a craving for adventures and inventions, alone of course. I am a student of many institutions offering different training and lessons in different fields. I seem stern at first glance but nicer when you get to know me, so I have been told. And this is a little about me.

I am currently a student of Mechanical Engineering at the University of Ilorin, Kwara State Nigeria. I had my primary education at the then Federal Polytechnic Offa Nursery and Primary School. And my secondary Education in Oyun Baptist High School Ijagbo and Adeola College Offa. I am an aspiring Aerospace and / or Robotics Engineer

I was born in the Ilorin Metropolis in October 2002 but grew up in the cities of Ijagbo and Offa. I was born to an Islamic Yoruba family of four children, me being the third. I am originally from Ijagbo in Oyun local government of Kwara State. I hold my Islamic-Yoruba identity to heart and I always seek to learn more about my heritage.

I am a science nerd. The physical sciences mostly. I am passionate about innovation, creativity, and Engineering. These passions have led me to fields like programming (mainly web), and graphic design (logo, UI/UX and recently 3D and Sci-Fi art). I love movies in the action, sci-fi, paranormal and fantasy genres, especially movies and series of the MCU. I am a die-hard Avatar: The Last Airbender fan as well.
